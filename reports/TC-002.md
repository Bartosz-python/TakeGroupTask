*Summary: [UI][Functionality] "Popularity" criteria stops working while either "Oldest Productions" or "Newest Productions" 
sorting criteria are applied in the Sort feature

*Description: After navigating to vod.film page and navigating to following subtabs with sort feature:
- Movies
- Series
- Popular
- Genre (any subtab of this option)
the popularity sorting criteria stops working while having either Oldest or Newest sorting criteria applied.

This issue has been reproduced on Chrome and Firefox on Windows 11 (10.0.26100).

*Steps:
1. Launch the browser.
2. Navigate to the "https://vod.film/" URL.
3. Proceed to the either subtab listed above.
4. Open the "Sort" feature.
5. Choose either "Oldest Production" or "Newest Production" sorting criteria.
6. Click the "Popularity" criteria.
7. Observe that the Popularity criteria stops working.

*Expected results: All respective sorting criteria can be chosen and filter the movies no matter what other critera are applied

*Actual results: "Popularity" criteria stops working while either "Oldest Productions" or "Newest Productions" 
sorting criteria are applied.

*Priority: Medium
