*Summary: [UI][Functionality] "Popularity" criteria stops working with "Oldest/Newest Productions" in the Sort Feature

*Description: After navigating to various subtabs (Movies, Series, Popular, Genre) and applying either "Oldest Production" or "Newest Production" sorting, clicking the "Popularity" criteria stops working.

This issue has been reproduced on Chrome and Firefox on Windows 11 (10.0.26100).

*Steps:
1. Launch the browser.
2. Navigate to the "https://vod.film/".
3. Proceed to the either subtab listed above.
4. Open the "Sort" feature.
5. Choose either "Oldest Production" or "Newest Production" sorting criteria.
6. Click the "Popularity" criteria.
7. Observe that the Popularity criteria stops working.

*Expected results: All sorting criteria should work independently or in combination. Selecting "Popularity" should filter and sort movies by popularity regardless of other applied criteria.

*Actual results: "Popularity" criteria stops working while either "Oldest Productions" or "Newest Productions" 
sorting criteria are applied.

*Priority: Medium
